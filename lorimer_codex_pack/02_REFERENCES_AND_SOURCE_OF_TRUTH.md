## Critical interpretation of the PDF
The PDF shows:
- repeated Lorimer logo anchor areas
- product rows functioning like editorial strips rather than standard grids
- mixed product cutouts and environmental images
- an alternating image and text system for the S/S24 section
- a lookbook logic with "View in Products"

This means the site should be treated as a lookbook first experience, not a standard store.

## Conflict resolution rule
If references conflict:
1. preserve the locked brand direction
2. prioritize spacing and mobile quality
3. prioritize the client requests
4. prioritize the Framer draft structure
5. use mfpen and buendia0222 as refinement references, not as copy pasted layouts
