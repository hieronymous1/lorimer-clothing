# 10_INTERACTION_MOTION

## Interaction principle
The site must feel editorial first and interface second.

## Allowed interaction language
- fade
- underline
- opacity shift
- subtle image swap
- quiet cursor hint

## Navigation behavior
- toolbar present only where needed, but on home it should disappear on scroll
- the text links under the Lorimer logo must be clickable
- internal pages should prioritize clarity without feeling UI heavy

## Products hover behavior
Client requested a charcoal transparent box with underlined "View".
Interpret this in the most restrained possible way:
- very light dark overlay
- underlined "View"
- no loud custom cursor theatrics

## S/S24 behavior
- "View in Products" must be clearly clickable
- arrow control for more images should feel light and precise

## Motion timing
- around 200 to 300ms
- no elastic motion
- no dramatic transforms

## What to avoid
- animation that calls attention to itself
- cursor gimmicks
- exaggerated scroll effects
- scaling on hover that cheapens the luxury feel
