# 14_IMPLEMENTATION_GUARDRAILS

## Do not do these things
- do not let the site drift into default Shopify or storefront styling
- do not use dense multi column product grids on mobile
- do not introduce random spacing values everywhere
- do not add unnecessary borders, pills, chips, or generic ecommerce filters
- do not over animate
- do not make the logo block hug the left edge
- do not leave filler text in the experience
- do not mismatch hover images and products
- do not treat S/S24 like a normal product listing page
- do not build asymmetry randomly

## Preserve these things
- black on white clarity
- negative space
- refined typography
- quiet interaction language
- image hierarchy
- editorial pacing
- mobile quality
- product imagery dominance on detail pages
- footer closure and About page credibility

## How to decide between two options
Prefer the option that is:
1. calmer
2. more spacious
3. more typographically restrained
4. stronger on mobile
5. less generic
6. more system consistent

## Final benchmark
The output should feel like:
- a luxury editorial storefront
- a quiet fashion archive
- a coded evolution of the Framer draft

It should not feel like:
- a template
- a simple Framer clone
- a default commerce implementation
