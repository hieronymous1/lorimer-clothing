## Core Positioning
Lorimer is not a standard storefront.
It should feel like:
- a quiet, precise, editorial commerce archive
- a luxury minimal fashion site
- a lookbook disguised as a store
- a curated product environment

It must not feel like:
- a Shopify template
- a startup minimal website
- a dense ecommerce UI
- a generic fashion grid

## Locked Design Direction
- ultra minimal
- black text on white background
- chic
- heavy on negative space
- editorial ecommerce
- strict editorial asymmetry
- typography led
- Lora is the preferred typeface
- Scandinavian minimal design language, but do not overplay Finnish or Nordic references

## Non Negotiable Principles
1. Spacing is the main design tool.
2. Mobile is recomposed, not resized.
3. Asymmetry must be controlled and repeatable.
4. Typography is the interface.
5. Images create hierarchy.
6. Motion must be subtle and nearly invisible.
7. The site must remain editorial first and commerce second.
