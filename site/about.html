Positioning

About

Lorimer works with clothing as archive material. Each release is treated as a chapter with clear cuts, reduced language, and deliberate spacing.

Method

The project stays editorial first: garments are documented as objects before they are sold as units.

Construction, fabrication, and fit are stated directly. Styling language is kept quiet so proportion and material stay in focus.

Studio

The studio operates between research and production, working in small runs with a preference for re-cut, layered, and modular forms.

The site structure mirrors that process: fewer interface gestures, more room for sequence, and stable routing between collection and product.

Practice

Three principles shaping the current system.

01

Sequence before conversion.

Pages are ordered like a lookbook so context arrives before transaction.

02

Space as hierarchy.

Negative space separates decisions. Type remains small and calm.

03

Object-level clarity.

Every garment is documented with material, fit, and cut notes, then linked back to seasonal context.
